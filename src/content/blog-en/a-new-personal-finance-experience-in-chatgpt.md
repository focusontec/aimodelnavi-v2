OpenAI has announced a preview of a new personal finance experience for ChatGPT Pro users in the United States. This brings AI-powered individualized financial consulting into a more concrete and personalized form.

## Secure Financial Account Linking
A key highlight of this update is the ability for users to securely link their financial accounts to ChatGPT. This allows the AI to provide "grounded" insights and guidance based on the user's actual financial situation, set goals, and priorities, moving beyond generic advice.

Previously, users had to manually input their income and expenses or upload CSV files for analysis. Account linking now enables seamless data utilization.

## AI-Powered Personalized Financial Insights
Here are the main features and experiences users can expect:

- **Situation-Based Analysis:** The AI will assess current asset status and spending patterns to suggest improvements.
- **Goal Guidance:** It will visualize progress towards savings goals or investment plans and suggest an optimal path.
- **Priority-Driven Advice:** The system will offer household management suggestions based on life events and values the user deems important.

## Availability and Conditions
This feature is currently available as a preview for **US-based Pro users**. Given the highly sensitive nature of financial data, OpenAI emphasizes robust security measures and operation within a secure connection environment.

There is no announcement yet about expansion to other regions, including Japan, or availability for free-tier plans. Nonetheless, this represents a significant step in ChatGPT's evolution from a general-purpose chatbot to a personal life manager.
